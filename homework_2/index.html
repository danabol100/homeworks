BMW 4 Series (BMW B4)

The BMW 4 Series is a sporty coupe and convertible that combines elegant design,modern technologies, and dynamic performance. It has been produced since 2013 and quickly became one of the most recognizable models of the brand. More you car read on official site >>

Design and Style

The latest BMW 4 Series stands out with its aggressive look, slim headlights, and the signature large kidney grille. Inside, it offers premium materials, a digital instrument cluster, and the advanced iDrive multimedia system.

Technical Specifications

The model is available with different types of engines:

  • petrol engines (from 2.0 to 3.0 liters),
  • diesel engines,
  • and even hybrid versions.

The top versions, such as BMW M440i, come with a 6-cylinder engine and the xDrive all-wheel-drive system.

Advantages of the BMW 4 Series

  1. Sporty character and strong dynamics.
  2. Modern safety technologies.
  3. Stylish exterior design.
  4. Comfortable and high-tech interior.

Fun Fact
